Division Notes

1.Division should be read from left to right.

2.If there is both division and addition/subtraction division should always be done first. And if there is multiplication you should usually still do division first

3.Divide one digit numbers by single digit numbers using repeated subtraction. For example, if you're asked to divide 18 by 3, you can subtract 3 from 18 until you can't subtract anymore, counting the number of times you were able to subtract

4.Use multiplication to check your answer. For example, if you found that 18 divided by 3 is 6, you can check your answer by multiplying 6 by 3, which should give you 18
